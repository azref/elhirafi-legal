This Privacy Policy is governed by Moroccan law, in particular Law No. 09-08 relating to the protection of individuals with regard to the processing of personal data. Any dispute will fall under the jurisdiction of the Moroccan courts.

3. Scope of Application

This Policy explains how your data is collected, used, and protected when you use the Al-Sanea Al-Hirafi mobile application. By using the Application, you agree to this Policy.

4. Data Collected

4.1 Data provided by the user

  • Account information (full name, phone number, email address).
  • Professional profile (profession, primary city, notification cities).
  • User-generated content (work requests, text and audio messages in the built-in chat).

4.2 Authentication (Google Sign-In)

We use secure authentication systems, including Google Sign-In. When you choose to authenticate using these third-party services, we only receive basic profile data (specifically your name and email address) from the service provider to create and manage your account.

4.3 Photos and Media

The application only collects the profile photo chosen by the user and audio messages sent via the built-in chat to facilitate communication between artisans and clients. The application does not collect or store any images of products or goods.

4.4 Location Data (Manual Selection)

The application does not use GPS or your device's location services under any circumstances. Location data (primary city and notification cities) are manually selected by the user from a predefined list built into the application. This information is used exclusively for the smart filtering system to connect clients and artisans in the same region.

4.5 Automatically collected data

We may collect:

  • Crash reports.
  • Device information (model, operating system, application version).
  • Technical logs for performance and debugging.

5. Purposes of Processing

Your data is used to:

  • Create and manage your user account (dual profile: artisan/client).
  • Filter and display artisans or work requests according to the selected profession and city.
  • Facilitate direct communication via the built-in chat system (text and audio).
  • Store and synchronize your data securely.
  • Improve the performance and reliability of the application.
  • Display advertisements (banners and interstitials).

6. Legal Basis

The processing is based on:

  • The express consent of the user.
  • The necessity for the provision of the matchmaking service.
  • Legitimate interest (improvement and security of the application).
  • Legal obligations where applicable.

7. Data Storage and Hosting

Local Storage: Your data (such as messages and audio files) is cached locally on your device to improve speed and reduce data consumption.

Your data is hosted via Supabase, which acts as a data processor according to our instructions. The data is hosted on secure servers. Data transfers outside Morocco are carried out in accordance with applicable legal guarantees and CNDP deliberations.

8. Data Sharing

We do not sell your data. We may share data with:

  • Supabase (backend services, database, and authentication).
  • Google AdMob (advertising services for displaying banners and interstitial ads).

9. Data Retention

We only retain your data for the time necessary to:

  • Provide the matchmaking and communication service.
  • Comply with legal obligations.

10. Your Rights and Data Deletion

In accordance with Moroccan law (Law 09-08), you have the following rights:

  • Right of access to your data.
  • Right of rectification.
  • Right of erasure (deletion).
  • Right to object to processing.

Account and data deletion: You can permanently delete your account and all associated data (profile, messages, requests) directly from the application by going to Settings > Request account deletion. You can also request deletion by contacting us at awrghise@gmail.com.

11. Security

We implement appropriate security measures, including:

  • Secure authentication (JWT).
  • Row Level Security (RLS) to ensure that your messages and private data are only accessible to you and your chat partners.
  • HTTPS/TLS encryption.
