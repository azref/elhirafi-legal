Account and Data Deletion

If you wish to delete your Al-Sanea Al-Hirafi account and all associated data, you can do so easily. We process these requests in accordance with Google Play's data safety policies and applicable data protection laws (including Law 09-08).

Method 1: In-App Deletion (Recommended)

The fastest and easiest way to delete your account is directly through the Al-Sanea Al-Hirafi application:

  • Open the Al-Sanea Al-Hirafi app and log in.
  • Go to Settings, then scroll down to the Account section.
  • Tap on Delete account permanently.
  • A confirmation dialog will appear. You will be asked to enter a confirmation word (e.g., "DELETE" or "حذف") to prevent accidental deletion.

Note: When using this method, a secure cloud function is triggered, instantly and automatically erasing all your data from our database.

Method 2: Deletion Request via Email

If you have already uninstalled the app and can no longer access your account, you can request data deletion via email:

  • Send an email to: awrghise@gmail.com
  • Email Subject: Account Deletion Request
  • Important: You must send this email from the exact email address registered with your Al-Sanea Al-Hirafi account to verify your identity. We may ask for additional identification if necessary to protect user privacy.

Email requests will be processed within 7 to 14 business days.

Data Controller Retention Policy

Zero Data Retention: Once the deletion process is executed (whether via the in-app cloud function or manually by us), the Data Controller (MOUJAHID ABDERRAHMAN) retains none of your data. All personal information, profile data, chat messages, and work requests are permanently and irreversibly erased from our servers.

Warning: This action is final and irreversible.

Once your account is deleted, you will lose access to your profile, chat history, and all connections with artisans or clients. Please ensure you have saved any necessary information before proceeding.
